About

Bolinao is a coastal municipality in the province of Pangasinan, the native language spoken is called Bolinao. It is a member of the Central Luzon branch of the Malayo-Polynesian language family. Approximately 50,000 speakers use this language primarily in the municipalities of Bolinao and Anda in Pangasinan.

Events

In Bolinao, a picturesque coastal town in Pangasinan, there are several delightful events and attractions that draw visitors. Let's explore some of them:

St. James Church: This historic church, built by Augustinians in 1609, stands as a testament to Bolinao's rich past. Despite the passage of time, St. James Church remains a remarkable architectural gem.
